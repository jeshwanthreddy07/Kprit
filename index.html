About KPRIT

Shaping futures since 2008

Kommuri Pratap Reddy Institute of Technology (KPRIT), established in 2008 by Sri Kommuri Pratap Reddy, is a premier NAAC A+ Grade (CGPA 3.31) accredited autonomous institution affiliated to JNTUH. Spread across 22 acres on the Hyderabad-Warangal Highway near Ghatkesar, KPRIT offers world-class education in engineering and technology.

With AICTE approval and UGC autonomous status, KPRIT is committed to fostering innovation, research, and industry-ready skills. Our NBA-accredited programs in CSE and ECE ensure the highest standards of technical education. EAMCET Code: KPRT.
